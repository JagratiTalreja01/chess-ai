AI & Chess

Why chess still matters in artificial intelligence

Chess remains a compact laboratory for reasoning, optimization, search, and strategic decision-making.

01

Minimax

Models adversarial decisions by selecting moves that maximize one player's outcome while minimizing the opponent's.

02

Alpha–Beta Pruning

Eliminates branches that cannot affect the final decision, reducing unnecessary search.

03

Heuristic Evaluation

Estimates position quality when exhaustive search is not computationally practical.

04

Planning

Connects immediate actions with long-term goals under changing constraints.

About the project

Built as a personal AI and strategy showcase.

The goal is not merely to display a chessboard. It is to create an interactive extension of a professional AI portfolio.
